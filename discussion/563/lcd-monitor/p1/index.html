  • TrueBeliever April 2008 Permalink
    Correct me if I'm wrong, but....

    Some of the newer monitors are trying to trick you. That samsung monitor you have up there is not really 2ms, it is 2ms(gtg) which is slower than 2ms(btb). btb (black to black) is the original method (and the most used method) of measuring ms, it measures the speed of going from black to white back to black. That takes a longer time than gtg which is just measuring for color to color. From what I read 2ms(gtg) is like 10ms(btb), I'm not sure how accurate that is but that is just something to keep in mind.
