  • Erling June 2010 Permalink
    Castle: More amazing stuff from Nathan (Mal in Firefly). Great show, good fun. Detective type show, but I like it more than any I've watched really.

    Sons of Anarchy: Biker gang show, really interesting lots of plot good characters.

    Battlestar: Gallactica: One of the, if not the, best show I've ever watched.

    Flash Forward: Canceled sadly as it was starting to pick up.

    Justified: Sort of a "cop show" version of Sons of Anarchy.

    Stargate SG1: Amazing Sci-Fi show.

    Stargate Atlantis: Even more amazing Sci-Fi show.

    Stargate Universe: Started off really slow, didn't get great hype but it's really starting to come into its own and looks to turn into a great series.

    The Tudors: Really interesting show about King Henry the somethingth. Lots of boobs if you're into that.

    V: Eh, good filler show, I haven't really found it too good.

    Band of Brothers: If you haven't seen this you really should!

    The Pacific: Band of Brothers in the Pacific, I found it lacking (when compared to BoB) but it's worth watching I would say.

    Southland: Fake Cops show, pretty interesting. Nothing too amazing here.

    Caprica: Telling the story about before Battlestar: Gallactica. I haven't been impressed by it really but it's interesting stuff anyway if you enjoyed BS:G.

    Firefly: Would say the second best TV show ever after BS:G. /tongue.gif" style="vertical-align:middle" emoid=":P" border="0" alt="tongue.gif" />

    Californication: Awesome show, just awesome. Tons of boobs since we all like that. David from X-Files really in his prime here if you ask me.
