    QUOTE
    Under the new system at level 70:

    It will take 115 int for 10 spirit to equal 5 mp5 out of the five second rule.(hunter, feral form druid, shaman warlock, paladin)

    It will take 142 int for 9 spirit to equal 5 mp5 out of the five second rule.(caster form druid)

    It will take 179.5 int for 8 spirit to equal 5 mp5 out of the five second rule. (mage and priest)

    At 235 int, 7 spirit equals 5 mp5 out of the five second rule.

    At 319 int, 6 spirit equals 5 mp5 out of the five second rule.

    At 460 int, 5 spirit equals 5 mp5 out of the five second rule. <- 1 spi = 1 mp5 out of the 5 second rule.

    At 718 int, 4 spirit equals 5 mp5 out of the five second rule.

    At 1275 int, 3 spirit equals 5 mp5 out of the five second rule.
