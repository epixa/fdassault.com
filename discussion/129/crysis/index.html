    Like Neo said, I'm just unconvinced when it comes to crysis. It looks amazing, I'll grant it that, but it's done by the same team that did Far Cry and despite the supposedly "redefining" nature of that game, I found it bland and generic. I'm worried that Far Cry will do more of the same.

    Plus, Crytek has succumbed to what I lovingly refer to as "Electric Monkey Syndrome" after the lopers in RTCW: essentially, it's using exponentially more difficult enemies to make things harder instead of coding better AI. And for all their talk of realism, nothing takes me out of immersion faster than having to shoot something with eight rockets to kill it.

    I will probably grab a copy when it goes on sale just to look at the pretties, but I'm almost positive I will not play through it unless it turns out it's something completely off the scale. With TF2 occupying so much of my time lately, the gameplay end is REALLY going to have to measure up to pull me away.
