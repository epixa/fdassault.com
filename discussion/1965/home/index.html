    Two different shooting quals that are added up for a total score.

    Table 1 is known distance, 200 yds, 300yds, and 500 yds. Total score can be up to 250 points. 190-208 or 209 (I forget the actual, might even be 205) is Marksman, 210-219 is Sharpshooter and 220-250 is Expert. Table 2 is shooting faster, doing box drills, etc. More combat oriented. 100 points total, so when added up 250-279 is marksman, 280-304 is sharpshooter, and 305-350 is expert.
