    As some of you may know, I've been writing weekly music reviews for my college paper and my own website that is currently being remodeled (but which you may frat's sexy design on) www.klap4music.com, and I've currently amassed nearly a 100 articles over the course of less than a year. I was recently approached to help with a music-review start-up site called Musebin. For those of you familiar with Twitter, the concept is similar, except it is totally aimed at music reviews. While I have never used Twitter, from my experience with Musebin (I've posted about 125 reviews so far), it is quite an awesome idea and I think it has the potential to take off. Below is a recent article about it and a link to the site itself; Musebin is still in beta but I do have seven or eight invites left. If anyone here would like an invite or is intrigued and would just like to check it out, PM me your e-mail or just write it here and I'll hit you up with an invite.

    For the record, I'm not getting paid or anything to do this, I just thought this would be a nice way to spread the word about something I personally think is pretty sweet. Let me know what you guys think.
