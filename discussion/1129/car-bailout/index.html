Car bailout
  • KP December 2008 Permalink
    I find it rather strange that no one here as really commented on the car industry bail out lately as it has been in the news a lot.
    It is rather an absurd concept to me. Since when has the government been horribly efficient? When has the government been good at fixing companies? And why should the government give private businesses money because they suck at business?

    Lately I have seen in the news is that in order to get the bailout money, they are going to be forced by the government to make some “hard decisions” with their business practices and turn their companies profitable….

    This statement really confuses me. It means that if the government wasn’t going to step in to solve their bad business practices they were going to keep doing what they were doing before. It means that if the government decided not to bail them out, that the car industries would be perfectly happy losing boat loads of money.

    A bailout isn’t going to fix their long term problems and it will be nothing but a quick fix in my opinion.
    And besides, I believe they can file for bankruptcy protection which will them even more time.
