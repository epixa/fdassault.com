London as a Pirate! YAR!
  • Funky Pa$ta T0mmY August 2008 Permalink
    For those who don't know, I am a huge fan of the London Circle line drinking game. There are 28 stops on the london underground circle line and you guessed it, they go aroud in a circle!

    The aim of the game is to get off at each stop and have a half pint at the nearest pub! A couple of weeks ago we did it in Pirate attire on a business day in London! Great fun, and just got some pics back for your viewing pleasure!




    My attire on the left!



    Me and my trusty parrot Polly! Sadly Polly was killed during the course of the day. I got drunk and ripped her feathers off. R.I.P Polly. As proven by this picture I clearly loved you.



    St James is near the end, so we must of been pretty smashed by then. The fact I don't remember this picture being taken probably proves that.



    Drawn on facial hair for the win!



    Here's to London! Best city on the planet!

    To those that haven't been to London, why the fuck not and when you do come, do the circle line drinking game! It be fucking immense! YAR!
