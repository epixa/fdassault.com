    Yesterday I was working at the YMCA and some kid didn't handle his iPod touch with care and had it stolen in the locker room. He came up to me and asked if I had seen it, I said no and decided to help him look for it. He was in two places, the weight room and the locker room. So I decided to ask everyone in those two room if they had seen a iPod laying around anywhere because the kid lost his. When I was in the weight room I went from the front of the room to the back asking people about the iPod. The last guy I approached who was in the back was black, the only black person in the weight room. He was wearing ear buds listening to music during his workout. I asked him about it and he had no clue about the iPod, so I left the room to continue the search in the locker room. About 15 minutes later I was called up to the front desk because there was a complaint about me. That black gentleman accused me of being racist, he said I singled him out because of his skin color. There is so many things I can say about that which pisses me off, but I just have to state how ignorant he is. He has his ear buds in listening to music not noticing that I asked every single person in the room before I asked him. Not only did I ask everyone, but I asked him last! He truthfully was the last person in my mind who would steal the iPod. There were plenty of teenagers there that I had more concern about then him. I really just wanted to know if anyone SAW the iPod, I wasn't accusing anyone. But anyway I guess what I am trying to say is that I am sick and tire of the boy who cried wolf.
