    We're hosting the first "official" FD Starcraft 2 tournament this upcoming Wednesday:

    Sign up: -- full --
    Date: 2011-01-05
    Time: 7:30pm EST
    Type: Double elimination, best of 1
    Teams: 8

    All skill levels are welcome. By referee decision, the favored side of hugely unbalanced match-ups in the winner bracket must play an off-race.

    Since we're trying to keep this to one night, the players will be strictly capped at 8. Our last tournament was in the same format with 6 players, and it ended up lasting close to five hours. To avoid that, multiple matches will be played at once, especially in the earlier rounds.

    For the sake of organizing the matches, the ongoing use of mumble throughout the night is required. Contact me if you need details for this.

    The last tournament was a ton of fun for everyone involved, and some of the most fun was had while chatting on mumble and observing other games. I hope we see some different faces this time as well!
