    QUOTE

    If it can be chalked up primary to player ineptitude and/or ego its not a "random" moment.
    When I say CS is random I refer to inconsistencies experienced during game play as a result of it being online. Any lag creates substantial inconsistencies in relation to hitboxes/shot registration. Playing in a perfectly objective and humble state of mind, all ego aside, will not change this fact. Even if you provided the most elite players (all in an objective and humble state of mind) with a perfectly configured/flawless LAN setup, all other factors aside etc.. you will still witness a substantial degree of inconsistency in what is displayed by the nano second on each players screen, whether it be where exactly a player is visually in relation to where the server says he is, or whos machine displays the other peeking around the corner first. Then theres always the shots when your xhair is far from on the person, or the misses when you are dead on etc..
    Noting these inconsistencies is a matter of sensitivity and how quickly you process new visual information. CS is getting old compared to the new stuff coming out. The issue of online play aside, the games are getting more cutting edge/faster. With CS you can end up waiting on the game, especially during online play.

    Like anything else people will use that technical "randomness" as an excuse...an excuse is an excuse.
