    The game is very good. By far the most stable and well polished mmo at release I've seen. *With that said, it still needs a lot of polish and touching up* But as a new mmo, I am very impressed at how well it is going so fresh off the press. The community in game is very hardcore. It's not for the light of heart. It takes a while to come to grips that you will die, and you will lose everything you have. Frequently. Once you can come to terms with that fact the game becomes very fun. For anyone who expects this to be a grind free game - unfortunately you will be disappointed. It requires huge grinds. Grinding resources is just hundreds of clicks over and over. In order to compete in pvp combat you had better grind your weapon skills. Magic is a literally endless grind. For me personally it doesn't really bother me, I don't mind grinds that much. For others I know it will be an enormous turn off. The game as of now is NOT a solo game. You definitely need a group of 3+ at any point that you want to pvp or even pve successfully. This is fairly disappointing to me, as I like to feel I can be productive on my own when I'm on at off times. Hmm... anything else I can answer I'd love to do so... Let me know if you have specific questions. As for my character at the moment, I am focusing on magic. I am grinding towards the Necromancy tree, which is a very very VERY large project... It will likely take a number of months to even reach. I have taken up enchanting and jewelsmithing as professions. Enchanting is very very difficult as of now. Anyway, as I said, feel free to ask if you have more questions.
