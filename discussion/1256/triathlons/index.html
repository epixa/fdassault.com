  • Phil March 2009 Permalink
    Yep, haven't done one since...04. I got a little bored with endurance sport, but the feeling of completing a good endurance event is something everyone should do at least once. Be it, triathlon, 100 mile bike ride (heres looking at you Tom), or marathon. Maybe not a lifestyle, but certainly worth doing once.

    You'll lose a bunch of your muscle, training for it, because its a different type of fitness.

    I suggest doing a sprint or Olympic distance one first. The Half Iron-Man is really grueling. And you can't compete in an Iron Man until you post a respectable Half time (they don't want people dying).

    Sprint = 400m swim, 10k bike, 5k run
    Olympic = 1.5k swim, 40k bike, 10k run

    Its quite the experience. Don't spend too much on your bike, and make sure you can swim pretty good!
