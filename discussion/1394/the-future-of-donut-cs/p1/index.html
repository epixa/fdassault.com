    I don't know if I should participate in this discussion since I wasn't really onboard from the start, but I really don't see this is a viable long-term (and by that I mean even a few months) venture. There are no more people [from this community or in the general cs community] playing CS now than there were a year and a half ago when we finally signed off on the old FD server, and of the people that do play, none that I'm aware of are able to actually schedule a significant amount of time to making the FD server happen.

    Of course, everyone could drop whatever games they currently play or hobbies they've picked up to replace the time they used to spend on FD to make the server a success again, but most of us will never be captivated to the same extent by CS again, so I think it is inevitable that the server population will decline over time regardless of how gung ho you all are from the start.

    The ultimate truth is, there are better games out than CS, and we don't play games nearly as much as we used to anyway. I would much rather focus on the actual alternatives that allow the members of FD to spend time together than engage in a pursuit of something that has little hope of longevity.
