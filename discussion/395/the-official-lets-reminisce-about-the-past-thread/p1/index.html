    So, as many of you know I have the wow bug. That being said I am in the same guild as Meadaball, Cannonfodder, and Fire from the old Tuff days. So tonight I was in the same vent channel as most of them and found our topic of conversation to be the old counter strike days. We went way back to the SmurF days and to the original Tuff members. We had a ton of fun thinking up old names of people we remembered and shared interesting stories.

    That being said, it made me miss Donut, and how it took atleast 10 minutes of constantly clicking refresh, waiting to see 15/16 slots. I vaguely remember epic Italy battles in smurf, and pretty much idolizing the ground smurf stood on. I was 14 at the time and so many of my friday nights were spent in these servers. We listed a number of old names and shared some laughs about each person. (Fishy, Aest, Barry, Green, Papa, Rafio, etc)

    So I open up the floor. Lets remember what donut/smurf/tuff etc meant to you back when cs was fun. I'm curious what our older members have to say, just because it was so long ago, i'm sure i didn't remember some of the better stories. I wish like hell the old smurf forums could somehow be viewed. Some incredible discussions took place, and i'd be interested to see exactly how naive i was. Everyone can post though, because we all have great stories.
