    Ok, well I was curious. Here's the way I understand the PvP though. Attacking someone unprovoked that you don't have some kind of aggressive stance towards, ie a clan you're at war with or an unfriendly race will lower your standing from neutral to evil, and this can happen in as few as two kills. Becoming evil gives all good and neutral aligned players and all NPCs the ability to attack you unprovoked. You can't use regular cities without great fear for your life. Your alignment is one of the very few things conveyed about your character when people put their cursor over you.

    I appreciate the PvP part of the game, and I think that it is part of what will make it a great game. However, I think that your "plan to kill anyone and everyone who stands in our way, has something i want and wont give it to me, because i feel like it, bored, or to defend what is ours and take what should be ours" method might have really dire consequences, especially in the beginning of the game.
