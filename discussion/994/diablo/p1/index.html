    QUOTE (Tom @ Oct 23 2008, 08:19 AM) <{POST_SNAPBACK}>
    I've never played any of the Diablos. Can someone enlighten me on what I'm missing?

    I can't speak for D1 much cause I didn't play it for long, but D2 is sweet.

    It's like WoW, but you can beat the whole game in an day if you're hardcore. You can take on like 50+ mobs at once, and skills are much more straightforward. The gear system is pretty cool. You can have a weapon with nothing special (grey), a weapon with a damage modifier like 'Deadly' which increases damage by some percentage (blue) (or any other number of modifiers like poison damage, increased min and max damage, increased durability, freezing, etc). There are set pieces which are green and, like most set pieces in most games increase in awesomeness as you collect the set. But perhaps the coolest fucking thing in the game is Rune Words. Shit drops stones called runes, which you can drop in socketed equipment. They do simple stuff like increase magic item drop rates or more damage to undead blah blah. But there's a huge list of "rune words". If you put the right set of runes into an item in the right order, then you get a ridiculous killing machine. As in +350% damage, +2 to all skills, +10% chance to freeze on strike, +10% to health and mana... it's badass.

    Also integral to the game is something called the Horadric Cube. You get it in Act II and it's a box with slots to drop items in. You use it to combine items together. For example, drop 3 Rubies in it and you get a better Ruby. Drop 3 of those in and you get an even better one. This is how you make the runes you need to make runewords, which is how you eventually rape face wherever you go. blahblahblahblahblahblahblahblahblah
