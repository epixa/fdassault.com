  • ebola March 2008 Permalink
    For counter-strike, the Icemat Siberias are actually better to pinpoint an enemy's footsteps. However, unlike the Barracudas, they are stereo, not 5.1 surround. If you're looking to use the headphones also for listening to music or watching movies, the Barracuda is a great choice. Otherwise, save some money, hear more accurate footsteps, and buy the Siberias.

    Also note: the Icemat Siberia's plastic casing is a piece of shit. It started falling apart within a few days. You can easily keep it together with some electrical tape (I think Phil was doing the same thing to his), but keep in mind that its about as sturdy as Hillary Clinton's records. The Razer on the other hand will take some punishment before succumbing to your abuse.
