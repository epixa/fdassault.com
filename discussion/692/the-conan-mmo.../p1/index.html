  • Brian August 2008 Permalink
    The starting area is amazing. For the first 20 levels, you think you may have found "it". The MMO to kill all MMOs. The one you could stick with for ever. Then after lvl 20, you leave the starting city. It is apparent after leaving the starting city that the rest of the game was RUSHED.

    I wish I could explain it better. All the effort, all the beauty, and all the "holy shit this game is good" is gone after you leave Tortage (the starting city). It turns into pointless grind, with pointless PVP. Player created cities do absolutely nothing, DX 10 is not available, all armor looks EXACTLY THE SAME, quests are unpolished and take on a familiar "go do this, come back and get money, and that's it" feel to them, with almost no extended story quests. Its honestly... a pathetic excuse for an MMO after level 20.

    There is also absolutely no endgame, in a game which takes a completely casual player 2 months to level to 80. End game gear is absolute crap compared to gemmed crafted gear... balance of raids is off, etc etc...

    And I know it is a new MMO, but to put it into perspective, I've played AO, DAOC, WoW, and AC2 at release, and this feels like the least polished and least finished of them all. Enough so that I quit because of the issues and design, which I haven't quit any other MMO for.

    Back to waiting for darkfall I go.
