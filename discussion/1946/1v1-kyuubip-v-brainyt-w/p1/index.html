    Nice game! Definitely showed a good strategy in this game, loved the hidden transition into void rays at the end to seal the deal!

    Also great use of unit upgrades, it made a big difference. It was good to see you take the 3rd expansion, extra income is always nice.

    WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER. WATCH TOWER.

    /smile.gif" style="vertical-align:middle" emoid=":)" border="0" alt="smile.gif" /> use them! They give map control and advance warning of enemy movement. You should always have a unit on the watch tower. The confusion mid game where you saw his army leave his base, and you didn't know what to defend (expansion or your main), could have all been avoided by one simple unit at that tower.

    Other major critique: Observers! You had a Robot Bay and didn't make any observers! Should have had them at expos/main to see what units he was producing, but also to give you the ability to control his income and know what he was up to. If you hadn't gone void rays (a good idea on your behalf, but you hadn't seen his tech for cruisers), those battle cruisers could have been the death of you!

    Also, you tossed some units periodically at his wall mid game, Im sure with the attempt to harass/see unit composition... but this is costly and doesn't give you much insight into the interior of his base. There are a few ways to scout as Protoss, the two most popular being:

    1. Observer! (Hey - if you already have a robot bay why not?)
    2. If you have sentry's with the hallucinate ability - cast it on a pheonix (super fast air unit) and fly it over his base.

    The intel gained from scouting is far more valuable than a couple hundred minerals!

    Other than that, solid play, lots of improvement but you definitely need to work on scouting - it makes all the difference in the game.
