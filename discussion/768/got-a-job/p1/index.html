Got a job
  • Cheezzypoof July 2008 Permalink
    Well I know some of you knew I was in school as an aspiring pilot. Since the airline problems I took the safer route and applied for an Air Traffic Control position. Took a test in March and got a 100 on it. This Monday I had an interview in NYC (for a job in Oberlin, OH) as an ATC for Cleveland Center. They offered me the job and gave me a tentative offer letter. As long as my background check passes I will receive a final offer letter in about a month. It takes that long because my security clearance will be top secret level.

    They are going to let me finish my last semester of school as well so I can graduate. So looking to start in late December or early January. Unfortunately this means 4 months in Oklahoma City at the FAA Academy before I go to my facility in Oberlin.

    But anyways, for those of you who were worried I would be your pilot, now you have to worry about me telling your pilot what to do.
