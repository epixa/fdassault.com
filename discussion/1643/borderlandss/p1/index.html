    I think the game is a lot of fun if you're playing it with friends. It isn't a finely tuned RPG though -- it is hilariously easy to cap your level (a week or two of quest game play or an hour or two of grinding with an already capped hunter), you could not possibly run out of gold if you tried, and you are basically always a couple levels ahead of whatever quest you are currently on.

  • Phil November 2009 Permalink
    I want to second everything Court and Lethal said, and add in a few things.

    Gun mechanics - precise and fun. What could you want? The shooting is good. Driving isn't so good, but who cares about driving.

    Customization - you can do enough of it to be fun, and because the game isn't dependent on min-maxing you can fuck around and have fun (also, virtually no respec costs) Nothing is bound to your character either, so I can loan Bill's alts my legendary guns etc

    As Court said, its not finely tuned, and perhaps tuned way too easy for the first play through (unless you have never played an RPG before and are trying to aim with your keyboard). The 2nd and 3rd play throughs are fun though as the enemies get harder.

    And, as Court said (on vent not on the forums) "I like this game, because its totally stress free". And it is, fuck around, or play hardcore, or farm or just do random stuff its all stress free, because its so damn easy :-D
