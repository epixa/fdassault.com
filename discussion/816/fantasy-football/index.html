  • BlackLight August 2008 Permalink
    Here's a quick outline. PM me if you have any further questions.

    We have a live, online draft that takes place whenever I set it as (preferably when as many people as possible can attend). Draft order is randomly chosen and each team/player drafts 17 players (in this league, you must draft at least 1 quarterback, 2 RB, 3 WR, 1 kicker, 1 defense, and 1 defensive player, along with reserves). If you can't make the draft, it will autopick for you. Each player makes points based on their performance in whatever week they play (i.e., a quarterback makes 1 point every 25 yards thrown successfully, 6 points for a TD, -2 for an interception, etc. you can check specific point totals on yahoo). Whoever scores the most points with their chosen players wins. You have to plan ahead for bye weeks and there's also waivers and such to add free agents, etc.

    It's a very easy game to pick up and leads me to compulsively watch every game on Sundays. You'll enjoy it
