    Just curious if anyone else remembers a few amazing game mess ups:

    Anyone remember the Gambler? He had some awesome tricks... one shotting roshan was always a treat, and even when they fixed that you could always just have the techies on your team and Ante Up on them and watch them explode all over kids for epic cash.     Man, good ol' DotA.
